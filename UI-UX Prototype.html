UI (User Interface) and UX (User Experience) design are essential aspects of creating user-friendly digital products. Figma allow me to make versatile designs that are visually appealing and interactive interfaces. With Figma, I could collaborate in real-time, making it easier to iterate and refine designs.
I have had experience with Balsamiq to quickly sketch out the basic layout and functionality of a user interface. Its simplicity and low-fidelity approach make it ideal for brainstorming and early-stage concept development.
By combining the power of Figma for high-fidelity designs and Balsamiq for initial wireframes, I can efficiently craft user-centered digital experiences that not only look great but also function intuitively, resulting in products that users love to interact with.
